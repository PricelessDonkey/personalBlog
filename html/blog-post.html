Why is it we cannot hold the positive, once it has been shown us? The answers are many, varying with school and dogma. In any case, do we not resemble those myopic individuals who, durin ga train ride in which they have fallen sound asleep, their spectacles safely in their pockets, suddenly open their eyes and, for a fraction of time, see everything sharp and clear, as sharp and clear as if they enjoyed perfect vision?
